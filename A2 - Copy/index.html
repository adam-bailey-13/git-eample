Hobbiest Photographer and Graphic Designer

I don’t leave the house without my trusty camera anymore with the fear that I might miss an amazing photo opportunity. Taking photos while I go out biking has been part of my (almost) daily routine for years now and I couldn’t be happier with the results.

About Me

Photography has been my major passion ever since I was a child. Seeing my sister always carrying her DSLR inspired me to steal my parents' camera and start taking photos of anything that piqued my interest. Being a kid, the subject of my photos were usually my action figures and my pets.
